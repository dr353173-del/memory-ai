🔒 Privacy Policy

Memory AI Pro v2.1

Developer: Deepak Rawat

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Memory AI Pro collects the following information when you use the app:

  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to the AI assistant for generating responses
  • Saved Memories: Personal info you share like name, age, work, hobbies
  • Chat History: Your conversation history for context in future chats
  • User ID: An anonymous identifier to manage your sessions
  • Feedback: Ratings and messages you submit through the feedback feature

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized AI-powered chat responses
  • To remember your preferences and information across conversations
  • To maintain your chat history for better context
  • To improve the app experience based on feedback

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this app:

  • Groq AI (groq.com): Your chat messages are sent to Groq's AI models (LLaMA, Gemma) to generate responses. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy
  • Render (render.com): Cloud hosting provider where the app is deployed

We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connection)
  • Data is stored in a SQLite database on our server
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
  • No payment or financial information is collected

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Memories: Use "Clear Memory" in app settings anytime
  • Delete Chats: Delete individual chats or clear all chat history
  • Delete Account: Request complete data deletion by emailing us or using the delete account feature in settings

When you delete data, it is permanently removed from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Memory AI Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided data, please contact us for deletion.

7. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

  • All chat responses are generated by Artificial Intelligence
  • AI may produce inaccurate or incorrect information
  • Do not rely on AI responses for medical, legal, or financial advice
  • Always verify important information with qualified professionals

8. Your Rights

  • Access your stored personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of all your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
